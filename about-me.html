Hello World!

My name is Aleksandar Andrijević. I am from Novi Sad, Serbia, and I am a software developer. Please feel free to explore the sections below to learn more about me.

From Childhood Curiosity to Software Development

As a kid, I found joy in playing basketball and spent 15 years honing my skills on the court. Alongside my athletic pursuits, I dabbled in the world of technology, experimenting with game creation in Scratch and building websites on platforms like Wix and WordPress. These early explorations sparked my curiosity and ignited a passion for the digital realm.

Exploring the Intersection of Design and Development

During my teenage years, I delved deeper into the world of design. I learned to harness the power of Photoshop, discovering the importance of aesthetics and the impact they have on user experiences. This fusion of creativity and technology captivated me, inspiring a desire to bridge the gap between design and development.

Merging Economics with Coding

While pursuing my studies in economics, I embarked on a parallel journey into the realm of programming. Intrigued by the potential of coding, I taught myself Python and embarked on a project to create a simple calculator. Which was a lot of fun. Unfortunately, I didn't continue at the time with learning more about software development, as I was already focuses on finishing my studies and basketball.

Embracing Change: From Economics to Software Development

Upon graduating with a Masters degree in Economy, I ventured into the corporate world, initially as a logistics officer and later as a sales manager in the automotive industry. However, during this time, I discovered that my true joy and fulfillment resided in the realm of software development. This realization prompted me to make a transformative decision to shift gears and pursue my passion wholeheartedly.

A Journey of Growth and Learning

Driven by a burning curiosity and an insatiable thirst for knowledge, I embarked on a journey to expand my technical expertise. I acquired a professional Scrum Master certificate from scrum.org, deepening my understanding of agile methodologies and effective project management. I immersed myself in programming languages such as C and explored the intricacies of web technologies, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

The Joy of Solving Problems

Coding and software development bring me immense satisfaction. The process of deciphering complex problems and watching my code come to life fills me with a sense of accomplishment. I find great delight in continuously learning and implementing new concepts, as it allows me to stay at the forefront of technology and embrace the endless possibilities it presents.

Embracing the Future of Innovation

Looking ahead, I am filled with enthusiasm for the future and the opportunities it holds. I am constantly seeking new ways to enrich my tech stack and challange myself.
